History

Morach&Morach Contadores started in 2016, and was founded by Ana Laura Morach and Adriana Melisa Morach. Given their extensive experience in the practice of accounting, they decided to get together to provide an outstanding and comprehensive service. Our team consists of specialist accountants, who deliver customized services to their clients. Currently, the firm operates in the provinces of San Juan and Mendoza in the Republic of Argentina.

Services

The main professional services provided by the firm are the following:

CONSULTANCY ADVICE AND CORPORATE SERVICES

  • Registration of foreign companies.
  • Auditing services.
  • Preparation of quarterly financial reports.
  • Accounting certifications.
  • Certificates of Increase of Capital Stock and others.

ADMINISTRATIVE, TREASURY AND FINANCIAL SERVICES

  • Control and verification of receipts.
  • Bank reconciliation.
  • Budgetary control.
  • Communication with suppliers, performance of payment orders for them and the execution of such payments.
  • Document organization and storage.

TAX SERVICES

  • Provision of advice and planning on tax and social security matters.
  • Filing of monthly tax affidavits to national and provincial bodies.
  • Monthly calculation and biweekly payment on account of withholding taxes.
  • Preparation and filing of annual affidavits to national and provincial bodies.
  • Mining Investment Act No. 24.196

SERVICES RELATED TO SOCIAL SECURITY AND LABOR

  • Provision of advice and planning on tax and social security matters.
  • Filing of monthly tax affidavits to national and provincial bodies.
  • Monthly calculation and biweekly payment on account of withholding taxes.
  • Preparación de reportes financieros.
  • Preparación de Libros Contables y Societarios.
  • Certificaciones contables.
